## Date

Permet de formatter les dates

        <p>{{ today | date:'dd/MM/yyyy'}}</p> <!-- 05/02/2017 -->
        <p>{{ today | date:'longDate'}}</p> <!-- February 5, 2017 -->
        <p>{{ today | date:'HH:mm:ss'}}</p> <!-- 13:48:43 -->
        <p>{{ today | date:'shortTime'}}</p> <!-- 1:48 PM -->
